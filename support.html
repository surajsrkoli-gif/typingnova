Support – Typing Nova

Get help with website functionality, technical issues, and making the most of your typing practice

Welcome to the Typing Nova support page. Here, you can find assistance for common questions and issues related to using our website. Our support is focused on helping you navigate the platform, troubleshoot technical problems, and make the most of our typing practice features.

Common Support Topics

Many questions can be answered quickly. Below are some of the most common topics users ask about:

Test Not Starting

The timed test (1, 3, or 5 minutes) or passage typing does not begin after you click "Start."

Timer Issues

The countdown timer behaves unexpectedly, runs too fast/slow, or doesn't reset properly.

Incorrect Results

The Words Per Minute (WPM) calculation, accuracy percentage, or error count seems inaccurate.

Text Not Loading

The practice passage does not appear on the screen or shows an error message.

Input Problems

The website does not register your keystrokes correctly during a test.

Mobile Issues

The typing interface does not work properly or is difficult to use on your device.

Try These Steps First (Self-Help)

Before contacting us, please try these simple troubleshooting steps—they often resolve the issue quickly.

  1. Refresh Your Browser: Simply reload the page (press F5 or Ctrl+R/Cmd+R).
  2. Update Your Browser: Ensure you are using an updated version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.
  3. Check Internet Connection: A slow or unstable connection can cause features to load improperly.
  4. Disable Browser Extensions: Temporarily disable ad-blockers or other extensions, as they can sometimes interfere with website functionality.
  5. Clear Browser Cache: Old cached data can cause display issues. Try clearing your browser's cache for this site.

Contact Support via Email
