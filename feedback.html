Typing Nova values user feedback. Your suggestions help us improve the typing practice experience. We encourage you to share your thoughts, suggestions, or report any issues you encounter.

How to Send Feedback

You can send your feedback directly to us via email:

Send email to:
feedback.typingnova@gmail.com

We read and respond to emails sent to this address.

What You Can Send

Website Suggestions

Ideas to improve the user interface, design, or navigation.

Bug Reports

Issues with typing tests, timers, error calculations, or display problems.

Feature Requests

Suggestions for new practice modes, tests, or functionality.

General Feedback

Your overall experience and thoughts about Typing Nova.

Privacy Note

We respect your privacy:

  • Email messages are used only to read and respond to feedback
  • Email addresses are not shared, sold, or distributed
  • No marketing emails will be sent to feedback senders
  • See our full Privacy Policy for details

Response Information

Feedback emails are reviewed manually. Response time may vary, and we may not be able to implement all suggestions, but each is carefully considered.

No Account Required

You don't need to login or register to send feedback. Sharing your thoughts is completely optional and accessible to everyone.
