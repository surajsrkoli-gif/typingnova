Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Typing Nova handles information when you use our typing practice services.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Typing Nova. We are a typing practice website dedicated to helping users improve their typing speed and accuracy through various practice modes and tests. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using Typing Nova, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Typing Nova is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email address unless you voluntarily provide it to us through contact forms or other means.

We may collect non-personal information automatically when you visit our website, including:

  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
  • Performance Data: Typing test results (speed, accuracy, errors) which are stored locally in your browser and not transmitted to our servers
  • Network Information: IP address (anonymized for analytics) and approximate geographic location

This non-personal information helps us understand how users interact with our typing practice tools and improve the overall user experience.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Typing Nova uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website.

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, such as maintaining your session during typing practice
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site, which pages are most popular, and how users navigate through our typing exercises
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for future visits
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show relevant advertisements (see Section 4 for details)

Cookie Control: You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some typing practice features on our website.

4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

Typing Nova uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense is a program that allows website owners to earn revenue by displaying targeted ads.

Here's what you need to know about advertising on our site:

  • DoubleClick Cookie: Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the internet
  • Personalized Advertising: Ads may be personalized based on your browsing behavior and interests
  • Non-Personalized Advertising: If you opt out of personalized advertising, you will still see ads, but they will be based on the content of the page you're viewing rather than your interests

Your Advertising Choices: You can opt out of personalized advertising from Google AdSense by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

We do not control the ads served by Google AdSense and are not responsible for their content. However, we strive to ensure that all advertisements on Typing Nova are appropriate for a general audience and relevant to typing practice and skill development.

5. Third-Party Services and Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. This Privacy Policy applies only to Typing Nova and does not cover the privacy practices of third-party websites.

When you click on links to other websites, we encourage you to read their privacy policies. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Third-party services we may use include:

  • Google Analytics for website traffic analysis
  • Google Fonts for typography
  • Font Awesome for icons
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) for faster loading times

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information that is collected through our website. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. These measures include:

  • Using HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security assessments of our systems
  • Limiting access to information to authorized personnel only
  • Storing minimal user data on our servers

Your typing practice data (test results, speed measurements) is primarily stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Typing Nova is intended for general audiences and is suitable for users of all ages who wish to practice typing skills. However, we are committed to protecting the privacy of children.

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other relevant regulations:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13
  • If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our systems
  • We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities and to help enforce this Privacy Policy by instructing their children never to provide personal information through our website

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will work with you to address your concerns and remove any such information from our records.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These may include:

  • Access: The right to know what personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: The right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: The right to opt out of certain data collection and processing activities
  • Data Portability: The right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used format

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have questions about our data practices, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

Local Storage: Your typing practice results are stored locally in your browser. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or cache.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page.

We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Your continued use of Typing Nova after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

Significant changes to this Privacy Policy will be communicated through prominent notices on our website or via email if we have your contact information.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please don't hesitate to contact us:

  • Website: Contact Form
  • Email: privacy@typingnova.com

We will make every effort to respond to your inquiry in a timely manner and address your concerns appropriately.

Thank you for trusting Typing Nova with your typing practice. We are committed to providing a safe, enjoyable, and privacy-respecting environment for all users.
